Food Paper Portugal, manufactures products such as: food paper, packaging for hot or cold food, recycled Kraft paper bags and personalized paper cups since 2005. While offering a wide range of disposable, biodegradable and completely compostable packaging for take away and delivery.

We are specialized in the integral development of personalized food paper and food cardboard paper packaging.

We look for the best raw material on the market such as: Virgin Cellulose Pulp paper, Kraft paper made from pure virgin fibers and recycled Kraft paper; as a result, achieving the kind of packaging that will take over the most demanding versatility for high fat content and moist food. All of this without neglecting the product's purity and elegant aesthetics for innovative concepts that require the type of packaging that vow's the brand's signature.

All manufacturing carried out by Food Paper Portugal are made with food paper and cardboard with the following certifications: ISO 9001, ISO 14001, ISO 22000, FSSC 22000, ISO 45001, ISO 50001, FSC® CoC, PEFC CoC, FDA/IMS Packaging Certification.

Meeting with our customers enables us to capture their ideas, embrace them, and elevate them. With the pursuit to manufacture the desired end product, personalized packaging, completely from scratch, with the measurements and colors desired.

Taking EU directives into account, measures were approved to promote a more sustainable use of resources and the adoption of circular solutions to reduce the consumption of plastic products. The Food Paper Portugal's team works to guarantee the maximum of ECO friendly solutions. Increasingly promoting the use of “environmentally friendly” packaging. Manufacturing it for Take Away and Delivery, in Food Paper, Food Cardboard or Kraft.

Recycled, Compostable and Biodegradable Packaging... Why?

The concern for the environment, it's a very relevant factor for the consumer and must be reflected in the creation and production of the packaging product for Take Away and Delivery.

When analysing the needs of nowadays consumers, which are more aware of the need to preserve the environment, manufacturers it's a must for manufacturers to help customers & brands to find the best suited materials for their packaging product, to replace materials of fossil origin with alternatives containing a lower carbon emission load, whilst also being renewable and recyclable.

In that respect, Take Away industries must address the urgent need to start manufacturing packaging with sustainable raw materials, such as virgin and recycled fibre cartons in order to give ecological solutions to various end goals, as well as the use of kraft paper for industrial use.

The current research facilities allow us to find options that respect the environment, and help with the conservation of our planet.

For some years now, water based and oxidized drying food paints have been used in food packaging. They allow us to make personalised changes in different types of packaging through a much cleaner and harmless way for the consumer.

All the materials used in the production and branding of Food Paper's packaging, contribute to a more sustainable and balanced consumption, Reducing our carbon footprint with the aim to increase the chances of a healthier human interaction with the ecosystem, aiming at preserving the environment without compromising its natural resources for future generations.

Thinking on recycling or contributing to a sustainable society, it's carrying and embracing the other, your family and yourself.

Paper Bags - Sustainability, innovation and more solutions

Food Paper Portugal is specialized in the production of a wide range of paper bags for the food industry and others. The design and development of our food paper bags is done in a personalized way, according to the highest quality standards, with custom models, certified materials and the best print quality.

Among several solutions, you can choose white kraft paper bags with handles, brown kraft paper bags with handles, bags with or without handles, custom-made food bags, paper bags for takeaway & food delivery, personalized food paper bags, reusable and biodegradable bags.

Compared to other materials, paper bags are the best option for the environment and today's consumers choose brands that meet more sustainable standards for transporting food. From certified paper bags, paper bags made with biodegradable raw material, ecological, to paper bags for takeaway and food delivery.

Paper bags, in addition to transporting and protecting products food products, can be personalized, in order to reinforce and promote the your visual identity.

Food Paper Portugal manufactures food paper bags, made to measure, with your image, valuing your brand and respecting the sustainability of the planet.
